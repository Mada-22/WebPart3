Our Journey

CakesRus was founded eight years ago as a small family-owned business built on a passion for baking. What started in the family kitchen with treasured recipes soon grew into a beloved local brand. Today we stay true to our roots: quality, care and a personal touch.

Meet the owners

Zubeida and Morne, a devoted married couple, have poured their passion and commitment into building and growing this business. Together, they create cakes and lasting memories for every special occasion.

Mission and Vision

We are dedicated to creating cakes that bring joy and celebration to every occasion. Quality ingredients, family recipes and personal service are at the heart of everything we do.
